All models used in Misery were originally licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution (CC:BY) License, which can be accessed here: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/.

These models have been transformed for use in Arma 3, with all due credit given to the original authors.

Misery mod retains the rights to these transformed assets. while Misery mod is not obligated to distribute these derivative files, they are available for use under the terms of the original CC:BY license.

If someone uses the models from this derivative work, they must provide attribution to both the original authors and to Misery mod (TenuredCLOUD) for the derivative work.

Please note that some files may have been removed from Misery mod. Despite this, we ensure that the current assets list is always up-to-date, reflecting the most recent changes and updates.

We are committed to transparency and adherence to licensing terms in all our operations.

Additionally, it's important to note that the names of the p3d assets may not match the original names of the asset models.

However, all necessary credits are provided, and following the provided links will guide you to understand the correlation between the transformed and original models.

We believe in maintaining a transparent and respectful approach to using and crediting original works.
